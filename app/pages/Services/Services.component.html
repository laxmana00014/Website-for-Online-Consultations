Our Services

At HealthPortal, we are dedicated to providing comprehensive healthcare services tailored to meet the needs of our patients. Our team of experienced and compassionate healthcare professionals is committed to delivering high-quality medical care with a focus on personalized attention and holistic well-being.

General Consultations

Our general consultations offer thorough health assessments and personalized treatment plans. Whether you need a routine check-up or advice on managing chronic conditions, our doctors are here to provide expert guidance and support.

Specialized Medical Services

We offer a range of specialized medical services to address specific health concerns. Our specialties include, but are not limited to:

  • Cardiology
  • Dermatology
  • Endocrinology
  • Gastroenterology
  • Neurology
  • Orthopedics

Preventive Care

Prevention is the cornerstone of maintaining good health. Our preventive care services include health screenings, vaccinations, and lifestyle advice to help you stay healthy and avoid potential health issues.

Diagnostic Services

Accurate diagnosis is crucial for effective treatment. We provide state-of-the-art diagnostic services, including laboratory tests, imaging studies (X-rays, MRIs, CT scans), and more, to ensure precise and timely diagnosis of medical conditions.

Telemedicine

Access healthcare from the comfort of your home with our telemedicine services. Schedule virtual consultations with our doctors, receive medical advice, and get prescriptions without the need to visit our clinic in person.

Wellness Programs

Our wellness programs are designed to promote overall health and well-being. We offer nutrition counseling, weight management programs, stress management techniques, and fitness advice to help you lead a healthier lifestyle.

Pediatric Care

We provide specialized care for infants, children, and adolescents. Our pediatric services include routine check-ups, immunizations, growth and development monitoring, and treatment of childhood illnesses.

Women’s Health

Our women’s health services cover a wide range of needs, including gynecological exams, prenatal and postnatal care, family planning, and menopause management. We are dedicated to supporting women’s health at every stage of life.

Senior Care

We offer comprehensive care tailored to the needs of older adults. Our senior care services include geriatric assessments, management of chronic conditions, and support for maintaining independence and quality of life.

Mental Health Services

Mental health is an integral part of overall health. Our mental health services include counseling, therapy, and treatment for conditions such as anxiety, depression, and other mental health disorders.
